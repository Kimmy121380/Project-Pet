Zoro is a cat. He has one eye blind but this minor impairment has not decreased his cuteness by one bit. He cries like a baby distinguishing himself from the rest of the feline family. Before he was really old, he tends to hide in the houses and you don't really see him often. But after he started living with Kimmy, he becomes a lot more socialable and hung out with crowds and sometimes said hi to the dogs when he passes by. Zoro smells like cleanax and all sorts of toiletries because that is the smell of his litterbox. He also likes hiding in beds and stay there whole day. It is always a pleasant to have Zoro smudge your face in the morning. He has a butthole resembling the shape of a flower. It has made many featured appearance on Ariana's notebook.

Biko is a German Shepherd. He has a huge head and long nose. He doesn't like anyone touching his paws and butthole. When you walk into the house, it is very likely he will poke your butt with his long nose and wiggle his tail to smack your legs by accident. When he pees uncontrolably on the floor, it usually creates a puddle. Fighting Bo and biting each other's heads have kept him occupied. Be careful when you are at a fire place with Biko because he might swipe his tail over the fire and be lit. Also wear shoes around him because he might drop a heavy log on your toes.

Aoife is a dog that resembles a sea urchin. She has heart murmur and a bad leg. She is basically blind and deaf towards the end of her life. She loves sitting on pillows and people's laps. Compared to Biko, she pees and poops in tiny portions. Her hair is fuzzy and black. Her eyes are green in the dark and murky. She has a diffcult time to get on top of the couches due to the shortness of her legs. Stitches on her head is the evidence that she had a brain surgery once. Leaves get stuck around her mouth and make her look like a vagabond. But she is loved no matter how untraditional her look may be.

After he started to receive insulin shots, he became more social around people. Bo has eaten many trash and leftover from a scavenger hunt of trashcan or around the house. He has eaten people's socks many times and had to go to a vet to retrieve the treasures stored in his stomache. He might have a weird fetish with Sheppard's stinky socks as the socks of entrapment were often his. His fur is always greasy but surprisingly Bo's body smell is tolerable and he is polite enough to not stink people with his breath like many other dogs might have.

Mimi is a cat. Once suspected to be a Devon Rex as an undeveloped stray cat, he has proven to be a more aggressive breed over time. His eyes are green like jade. Amongst his many talents, opening water faucet and pulling washing machine's tubes to flood Kimmy's balcony for a number of times are the most memorable. Though being naughty, he is moderate with his eating. Unlike many other cats, eels and crab meat have eluded his attention, yet fried chicken bone scored his choice of favor. Showers have not salvaged the fate of his white fur turning yellow. He passed away on a very cold day of winter. The loss is more than words and tears.
